Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

Welcome to Rizz me UP (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information from and about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and any profile information you provide (e.g., name or nickname).
  • Profile Context: You may provide personal details such as hobbies, traits, and other context to personalize the AI-generated results.
  • User Content: When you use the App to generate conversation starters, you may upload screenshots of conversations or profile pages. These images are processed in real-time by AI and are NOT stored permanently on our servers.
  • Support Requests: When you contact us for support, we collect the information you provide in your message, including your email address.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with the App, such as the features you use, the "rizz" styles you select, and the frequency of your generations.
  • Device Information: We collect standard information about your mobile device, including its model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and App version.
  • Location Data: We may collect general location data (based on your IP address) to understand our user base.

C. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use standard technologies (like tokens and local storage) to keep you logged in and remember your preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's features (including AI-powered analysis).
  • Personalize your experience based on your profile and selected styles.
  • Manage your subscriptions and credits.
  • Improve our App and develop new features.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure the security of our services.

3. Third-Party Services

We use several third-party services to help us operate our App. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase: Used for authentication, database storage (Firestore), cloud functions, and App Check (security).
  • Google Analytics: Used to understand App usage and performance.
  • Google Gemini AI: Used to analyze your uploaded content and generate conversation starters. Your content is sent to Google's servers for processing.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • Brevo: Used to send support emails when you contact us through the App.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request the deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Opt-out of certain data collection or processing.
